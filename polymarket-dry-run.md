### Top Strategies

1. **Cross-Platform Arbitrage** -- Buy YES on one platform and NO on another when combined cost < $1.00. Tools like arbs.xyz scan for spreads in real-time. One trader made $6K in 30 days purely from arbitrage without predicting any outcomes.

2. **Mention Market Specialization** -- Trading on what specific speakers (Trump, TV hosts, earnings calls) will say. Top traders use tools like MentionMetrics and EventQuant to model word frequency from past transcripts, then combine with current event context. Understanding grammar rules (open vs. closed compound words) created a single $60K trade.

3. **15-Minute Crypto Scalping** -- Trading BTC/ETH/SOL 15-min up/down candle markets. Wait until last 1-2 minutes for confirmation, bet on direction. Avoid low-volume "doji" candles. Generates volume for airdrop farming as a secondary benefit.

4. **Niche Specialization** -- Find 1-2 categories you know deeply and stick to them. Track P&L by category and ruthlessly cut losers. One trader is up $1,570 in sports but down $400 in culture.

5. **Correlated Hedging** -- In mention markets, identify correlated words (e.g., "hoax" and "climate change") and take opposing positions to lock in profit regardless of which one hits.

### Common Mistakes

- Chasing markets outside your niche on impulse
- Top-blasting after a news event when the market has already repriced
- Not reading platform rules (same event can resolve differently on Kalshi vs. Polymarket)
- Doubling down on losers instead of accepting new information
- Copy-trading someone who entered at 40c when price is now 65c
- Betting without quantified expected value
- Using market orders without understanding order book mechanics

### Risk Management Takeaways

- Only 7.6% of Polymarket wallets are profitable
- Never risk more than you can afford to lose
- Use fractional Kelly Criterion for position sizing
- Nos are structurally safer than Yeses (fewer conditions needed)
- Almost always use limit orders (bid), not market buys
- Don't bid more than 24 hours before resolution
- Polymarket has no native stop-loss -- bot builders must implement their own
- Monitor White House press pool for real-time context on mention markets
