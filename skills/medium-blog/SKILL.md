# Medium Blog Writing Skill

## Purpose

Write long-form blog posts for Crevita's Medium publication covering AI safety, ethics, and emerging technology. Posts target a broad, inclusive audience with a commitment to making complex AI research accessible regardless of educational background.

## Brand Identity

Crevita is an independent voice covering AI topics including companies (Anthropic, OpenAI, Google) that create the tools she uses. When covering these companies, include explicit conflict-of-interest disclosures near the bottom of the piece.

## Post Structure

Every blog post follows this format:

1. **Strong Opening Hook** -- Grab attention with a concrete, dramatic scenario rather than abstract explanations
2. **What Was Discovered** -- Core findings in plain language with data/statistics
3. **Why This Matters** -- Context, significance, and implications
4. **Reality Check** -- Limitations, counterarguments, balanced perspective
5. **What Needs to Happen** -- Stakeholder-specific actionable recommendations
6. **The Bottom Line** -- Concise, measured summary
7. **Citations and source links**
8. **COI disclosure** (when applicable)
9. **Cross-links to related Crevita posts**

## Writing Rules

- Target approximately 8th-grade reading level
- Replace jargon with plain language and concrete analogies
- NEVER use em dashes ("--" instead)
- No fabricated links -- cross-references must use real, verified URLs
- Balance over alarm -- conclusions should be measured and informative, not preachy, judgmental, or alarmist
- Titles must use "hook point" techniques (curiosity gap, pattern interrupt, or data-forward)

## "What Needs to Happen" Guidelines

Use distinct, non-redundant stakeholder categories:
- AI Model Developers
- Researchers/Policymakers
- Business Leaders/Employers
- Everyday Users (always included, even in technical posts)

Format: Bold stakeholder headings, 3-6 concrete bullet points each, action verbs with measurable targets, 200-400 words total.

Tone by post type:
- **Crisis/lawsuit posts:** Emphasize company accountability
- **Research/technical posts:** Focus on industry best practices
- **Safety/policy posts:** Balance all stakeholder perspectives

## Marketing Assets (per post)

1. **Article description:** 100-character hook-style (curiosity gap, pattern interrupt, or data-forward)
2. **LinkedIn caption:** 4 lines max, ends with CTA to Medium link, includes #Crevita hashtag. Curiosity-gap approach -- withhold the payoff to drive clicks. Brief, cliffhanger-style, not comprehensive summaries.
3. **X/Twitter post:** Under 280 characters, hook-style, link to Medium article
